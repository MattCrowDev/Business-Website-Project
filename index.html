What is East-Tec?

Eastern Tecnoligy (East-Tec) is a educational startup dedicated to providing innovative programs that prepare young adaults to enter the workforce and pursue continuing education. East-Tec programs provide campers opportunities to advance their education in a less traditinal more exciting enviorment that includes hands on learning with professionals in the field.




What we offer

The goal of East-Tec is to provide a unique learning experience that is tailored to the individual camper. We offer both a one week interest program, and a one month deep dive program. The interest program is where the students will be exposed to new broad concepts to create interest and a desire to learn and push more into the field. Our longer deep dive program lets a student not only be exposed to these concepts but get the chance to take a closer look, creating their own projects over the course of the program.




Our standards

Cleanliness

A clean camp promotes a comfortable work environment and suggests a first impression of organization and professionalism. Camper health is also a reason to regularly remove dust and bacteria. Cleaning may be performed as frequently as every day, depending on the size and use of the camp space.


safety

Safety is a core corporate value at East-Tec. Our Safety Policy directly reflects the camps commitment to safety in the workplace, operations, and a positive safety culture. This policy applies to all personnel and every aspect of the camps activities. It is East-Tec policy to identify and comply with all applicable federal and state laws regarding workplace safety, while striving to incorporate industry safety â€œbest practices."

East-Tec utilizes a Safety Management System (SMS) to reduce risk of injury to campers, to prevent accidents, minimize damage to equipment and property, and to work proactively to identify and reduce the existence of hazards and risk in the work environment. To that end, we continuously examine our operation for those hazards and find ways to minimize them.
